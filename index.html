Porridge

Perfect porridge for a healthy way to start the day.

  • 40g oats
  • 200ml milk or water, or a mixture of the two
  • 1 tablespoon of raw honey
  • 1 handful of berries(blueberries, blackberries, raspberries)
  • cinnamon powder
  1. Put the oats into a pot and add the milk
  2. Use medium heat and stir the the content
  3. After the mass reeaches the desired consistence, put it onto a plate
  4. Mix the honey under
  5. Sprinkle the cinnamon over the porridge
  6. Finally add the berries on top and voil√† the enjoy your porridge.
